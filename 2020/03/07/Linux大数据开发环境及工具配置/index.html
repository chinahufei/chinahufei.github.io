  • 下载地址: https://www.scala-lang.org/download/

Hadoop

  • winutils.ext下载地址: http://public-repo-1.hortonworks.com/hdp-win-alpha/winutils.exe
  • hadoop.dll下载地址: https://github.com/steveloughran/winutils/tree/master/hadoop-2.7.1/bin
